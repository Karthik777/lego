## The Scriptures

We believe the Bible, in its entirety, is the inspired and authoritative Word of God — the final rule for faith
and life.

## God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

## Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is fully God and fully man, born of a virgin, who lived a sinless life, died on the cross
for our sins, rose bodily from the dead, and ascended to the right hand of the Father, where He now reigns.

## The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit indwells every believer, gives assurance of salvation, and empowers a life that
reflects Christ.

## Humanity and Sin

We believe all people are created in the image of God but are separated from Him by sin, and are in need of the
salvation that only Jesus Christ provides.

## Salvation

We believe salvation is a free gift of God's grace, received through faith in Jesus Christ alone — not by works —
and results in new life, forgiveness of sin, and adoption into God's family.

## The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, made up of all who trust in Him. We are not a replacement for the
local church — we encourage every believer to be rooted in a local church community wherever one is available.

## The Return of Christ

We believe in the personal, bodily return of Jesus Christ, and the final restoration of all things.

## Eternity

We believe in the bodily resurrection of the dead — the saved to eternal life with God, and the lost to eternal
separation from Him.
